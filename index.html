OVERVIEW

What is 12 Testers – Testers Community?

12 Testers – Testers Community is a simple, secure platform where developers publish apps for closed testing and testers earn tokens by testing those apps. It is built to streamline Play Store closed testing: developers upload a test listing (icon, Play test link, Google Group), spend tokens to get testers, and get real feedback. Testers follow the tasks (install → open → feedback → claim tokens).

Key benefits

  • Fast closed-testing flow that integrates with Play Store testing links and Google Groups
  • Token-based reward system to incentivize real testers
  • Automated tracking of task completion (open app, feedback, collect tokens)
  • Safety & moderation rules to avoid abuse
FEATURES

Features — Step by step

Publish an app (Developer)

Upload title, description, icon, Play closed-testing link and Google Group. Pay tokens to list your app for 24 hours to attract testers.

Discover & Test (Tester)

Browse home feed without login. To test or claim tokens you must log in. Follow the testing steps to earn tokens.

Task Tracking

Automatic step tracking: test-now → open app → submit feedback → collect tokens. Each step is recorded in Firestore for fairness.

Token Economy

Tokens are awarded on successful completion (e.g. +10 for download, +15 group join, +20 feedback, +25 rating — configurable).

Notifications

Developers receive push notifications when someone tests their app (you can enable this in your account).

Safety & Moderation

Uploads are moderated. Prohibited content is removed and accounts violating rules are blocked.

HOW IT WORKS

Flow (Developer → Tester)

  1. Developer: Creates a listing (title, description, icon), adds closed-testing Play link + Google Group, pays tokens to publish for 24 hours.
  2. Home Feed: Listing appears on home feed for 24 hours; testers see it without login.
  3. Tester: Clicks Test Now → Play Store opens to install the test app; tester performs tasks (open app, give feedback, play).
  4. Verification: App records steps to Firestore (openedApp, gaveFeedback, rewarded) — each step is validated (Play store fallback if needed).
  5. Rewards: After completing tasks, tester taps Collect Tokens and receives tokens in their Firestore user doc (transaction-safe).
  6. Developer notification: When a tester completes, the developer receives a notification (push/cloud function trigger).

Note: Listings auto-delete after 24 hours to keep the feed fresh and promote fairness.

TOKENS & PAYMENTS

Tokens are the internal currency. Developers spend tokens to publish; testers earn tokens by completing tasks. Tokens are tracked in your Firestore user document and updated with secure transactions to avoid race conditions and fraud.

FAQ

Common questions

Do testers need to login?
Yes — viewing listings is open, but to claim tokens testers must sign in.
How long is a listing visible?
24 hours (auto-delete) — this helps freshness and equitable exposure.
Can I cancel a listing?
Not from the UI yet; listings expire automatically. Contact support if immediate removal is needed.
