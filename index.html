Dynamic Two-Tier Upload & Reward System

Developers choose their upload tier for guaranteed visibility. Testers are rewarded based on the effort and premium status of the app they test.

Standard Listing

The Community-Driven Tier: Earn and Upload.

Developer Cost: **100 Tokens** to list the app for 24 hours.

Visibility Logic: **First-come, first-served**. The app is visible on the home screen, but any new upload (100T or 5000T) will push it down.

Tester Reward: Testers earn **25 Tokens** per completed test. (4 tests = Free 100-Token Upload).

Premium Listing

Maximize Exposure: Stay at the Top for 24 Hours.

Developer Cost: **5000 Tokens** to secure a top-ranked slot.

Visibility Logic: **Top Position Guaranteed** for 24 hours. Your app remains above all Standard (100T) listings. (Newest 5000T app is always highest).

Tester Reward: Testers earn **50 Tokens** per completed test, incentivizing rapid testing for your premium app.

Note: All apps, regardless of tier, are automatically removed after the 24-hour cycle to maintain a fresh, manageable testing pool.

The Verified 4-Step Testing & Token Flow

  • 1

    Test Now → Open Play Store

    The tester is redirected to the Google Play Store Close Testing link (External Link & Google Group Join) provided by the developer.

  • 2

    Open App (Launch Installed App)

    Once installed, the button changes to "Open App," allowing the tester to launch the installed application and begin the testing session.

  • 3

    Give Feedback (Verified Review)

    The tester submits their required review/feedback on the Play Store, fulfilling the core requirement for the developer.

  • 4

    Collect Tokens (Dynamic Reward)

    Reward is automatically credited: **25 Tokens** for Standard apps, or **50 Tokens** for Premium (5000T) apps.

Core Application Modules & Logic Applied

1. Home Screen: Dynamic Listing & Auto-Cleanup

  • Priority Listing: 5000T apps always rank above 100T apps. Newest app is always at the top of its respective tier.
  • **Strict 24-Hour Cycle:** All listed apps are automatically deleted from the feed after 24 hours to ensure rotation and focused testing.
  • Wallet Token counter, App List (Icon, Name, Developer, Credits) and Upload Button are always accessible.

3. Download / Test Screen: Information Hub

This screen hosts all app details and the multi-step flow. The button's text and function change based on the tester's action, guiding them through the test, install, review, and token collection process.

2. Upload Screen: Dual-Option Listing

Developers access a form requiring crucial details:

  • App Name, Developer Name, Icon, Description.
  • **Critical URLs:** Google Play Testing URL & Google Group Join URL.

Upload Pop-up Options:

  • Option 1: **100 Tokens** (Standard Listing)
  • Option 2: **5000 Tokens** (Premium Top Listing)
  • Limit: Only **1 app per developer per day** is allowed.

4. Profile Screen: User & App Management

  • User Name, Email, Token Balance, and Edit Profile functionality.
  • **My Apps Dashboard:** Separate views for "Apps Uploaded" (developer view) and "Apps Downloaded/Tested" (tester view).
  • Settings and Secure Logout options.
